The Starship Rocket Is Successfully Landed!

after a 115 days long journey the crew of 12 astronauts finally arrived to their destination.
This is the first time humans have set foot on a planet other than earth.
The starship rocket was built by nasa in california laboratory.
It is claimed to be the most advance rocket made by humans in the history as well.
NASA spent about $30 billion and launched it with 12 expert astronauts on 1/april/2023.
it took 5 years of hardwork of scientists and a lot of money and time as well.
